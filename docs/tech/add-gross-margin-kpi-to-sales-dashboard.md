# Add Gross Margin KPI to Sales Dashboard
## Overview
This Business Requirements Document (BRD) outlines the need to integrate a Gross Margin Key Performance Indicator (KPI) into the existing Sales dashboard. This enhancement will provide users with a comprehensive view of sales performance by including gross margin metrics alongside existing sales data.
## Description
The Sales dashboard currently provides insights into revenue, sales trends, and other key metrics. To enable deeper financial analysis, a Gross Margin KPI must be added. This KPI will calculate and display the gross margin percentage based on revenue and cost of goods sold (COGS) data. Users will be able to track gross margin trends over time and gain actionable insights into profitability.
## Acceptance Criteria
- The Sales dashboard must display a new Gross Margin KPI widget or section.
- The Gross Margin KPI must calculate and display the gross margin percentage based on revenue and cost of goods sold (COGS) data.
- Users must be able to view gross margin trends over time (e.g., daily, weekly, monthly, quarterly).
- The Gross Margin KPI must update automatically when underlying sales or COGS data is refreshed.
- The KPI must be visible to all users with access to the Sales dashboard without requiring additional permissions.
- The Gross Margin KPI must include tooltips or documentation explaining how it is calculated.
