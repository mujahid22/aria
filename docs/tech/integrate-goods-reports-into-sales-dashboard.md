# Integrate Goods Reports into Sales Dashboard
## Overview
This Business Requirements Document (BRD) outlines the need to enhance the sales dashboard by integrating a goods reports section. The goal is to provide users with a unified view of sales data and goods-related metrics, such as inventory levels, stock performance, and product analytics. This integration will enable comprehensive analysis and better decision-making.
## Description
The sales dashboard currently displays sales-related data but lacks visibility into goods reports, such as inventory levels, stock performance, and product metrics. This enhancement will add a dedicated section to the dashboard to display these goods reports alongside existing sales data. The integration will ensure that users can analyze sales and inventory data in a single view, improving operational efficiency and insights.
## Acceptance Criteria
- Goods reports section is visibly integrated into the sales dashboard layout.
- Reports display real-time inventory and stock level data for all products.
- Product performance metrics (e.g., sales velocity, turnover rate) are included in the reports.
- Data in goods reports is aligned and comparable with existing sales data in the dashboard.
- Users can filter or toggle between sales data and goods reports without leaving the dashboard.
- The system updates goods report data at the same frequency as sales data (e.g., daily or real-time).
## Priority
High
