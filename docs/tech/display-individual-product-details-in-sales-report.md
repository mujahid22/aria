# Display Individual Product Details in Sales Report
## Overview
This Business Requirements Document (BRD) outlines the need to enhance the existing sales report feature to include detailed information for each individual product. This enhancement aims to provide deeper insights into product performance by displaying key metrics such as product name, ID, quantity sold, unit price, and total revenue generated.
## Description
The current sales report feature provides a high-level overview of sales performance but lacks detailed information about individual products. To enable better decision-making, the sales report must be enhanced to include a dedicated section for individual product details. This section will display critical metrics for each product, including:
- Product name and ID
- Quantity sold
- Unit price
- Total revenue generated
Additionally, the feature must support filtering, sorting, and accurate calculations to ensure usability across all report formats.
## Acceptance Criteria
- Sales reports must include a section for individual product details.
- Each product entry must display: product name, ID, quantity sold, unit price, and total revenue.
- The product details section must be clearly labeled and easily readable in the report.
- The system must allow filtering or sorting of products in the report (e.g., by revenue or quantity sold).
- The report must accurately calculate and display the total revenue for each product.
- The feature must work for all existing sales report formats (e.g., PDF, CSV, and dashboard views).
## Priority
High
