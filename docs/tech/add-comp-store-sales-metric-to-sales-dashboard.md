# Add Comp Store Sales Metric to Sales Dashboard
## Overview
This Business Requirements Document (BRD) outlines the need to enhance the sales dashboard by introducing a 'Comp Store' (Comparable Store Sales) metric. This addition will provide stakeholders with critical insights into the performance of comparable stores, enabling data-driven decision-making.
## Description
The sales dashboard currently lacks a dedicated metric for Comparable Store Sales (Comp Store Sales). This metric is essential for evaluating the performance of stores that have been open for at least 12 months, allowing for accurate year-over-year comparisons. The requirement involves:
- Adding a 'Comp Store Sales' metric to the dashboard.
- Including visualizations such as line charts, bar charts, or tables to display trends and comparisons.
- Enabling users to filter or segment data by time periods (e.g., weekly, monthly, quarterly).
- Ensuring the metric adheres to predefined business rules (e.g., stores open for at least 12 months).
- Providing comparisons against non-comparable stores or industry benchmarks if applicable.
- Ensuring the dashboard updates dynamically with new sales data.
## Acceptance Criteria
- The 'Comp Store' metric is displayed prominently on the sales dashboard.
- Visualizations (e.g., line charts, bar charts, or tables) are included to show Comp Store Sales trends over time.
- Users can filter or segment Comp Store Sales data by time periods (e.g., weekly, monthly, quarterly).
- The metric accurately reflects Comparable Store Sales based on predefined business rules (e.g., stores open for at least 12 months).
- Visualizations include comparisons against non-comparable stores or industry benchmarks if applicable.
- The dashboard updates dynamically when new sales data is available.
