# Update Net Sales Formatting in Sales Dashboard
## Overview
This Business Requirements Document (BRD) outlines the need to enhance the visual presentation, clarity, and consistency of net sales formatting in the sales dashboard. The goal is to improve readability and user experience without altering the underlying data or calculations.
## Description
The sales dashboard currently displays net sales data in a format that lacks visual consistency and clarity. This initiative aims to standardize the formatting of net sales values, including currency symbols, decimal places, fonts, colors, and alignment. The updates will ensure a cohesive and professional appearance while preserving the accuracy of the underlying data.
## Acceptance Criteria
- Net sales values must display in a consistent format (e.g., currency, decimal places) across all dashboard sections.
- Visual elements like fonts, colors, and alignment must be standardized for net sales data.
- The updated formatting must not alter the accuracy or values of the underlying net sales data.
- The dashboard must maintain responsiveness and performance after formatting changes.
- Stakeholders must review and approve the new formatting for clarity and consistency.
- Any tooltips or labels associated with net sales must be updated to reflect the new formatting.
